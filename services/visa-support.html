Visa Support

Complete support for your Chinese student visa (X1/X2) — from paperwork to your embassy appointment.

With your admission letter secured, the student visa is the final gate before departure — and embassies reject incomplete files without explanation. We make sure yours is complete, consistent, and submitted correctly.

Your advisor prepares the full visa file with you: the JW202 form, admission letter, medical examination, financial documents, and the application itself. We brief you before your embassy appointment so there are no surprises.

What's included

1Visa file preparation

Every required document assembled and checked: JW202, admission letter, medical form, financials.

2Application filing

The visa application completed correctly and the appointment booked at the right consulate.

3Interview briefing

A preparation session covering exactly what the embassy will ask and check.

4Contingency support

If anything is questioned or delayed, we respond with the embassy directly alongside you.

How it works

  1. We assemble your visa file the day your admission letter arrives.
  2. Medical examination and financial documents are completed to spec.
  3. We book and prepare you for the embassy appointment.
  4. Your passport comes back with your student visa.

This service is part of our end-to-end journey — see all five services on the homepage services section.
