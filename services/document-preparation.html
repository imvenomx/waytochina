Document Preparation

We prepare, translate, and verify every document your application requires — accurate, compliant, and on time.

Most rejected applications fail on paperwork, not on grades. Chinese universities and scholarship committees expect precisely formatted, translated, and notarized documents — and every program has its own checklist.

We take this entirely off your plate. Your advisor builds your personal document checklist, we handle certified translations and notarization guidance, and every file is double-checked before submission so your application is flawless the first time.

What's included

1Personal checklist

A program-specific list of every required document, with deadlines — nothing missing, nothing extra.

2Certified translation

Arabic and French documents translated to English or Chinese by certified translators.

3Notarization guidance

Step-by-step help getting diplomas and certificates notarized and legalized correctly.

4Quality review

Every file verified twice — format, naming, validity dates — before anything is submitted.

How it works

  1. Receive your personalized document checklist.
  2. Send us scans of your documents — we review them within 48 hours.
  3. We manage translations, corrections, and notarization guidance.
  4. Your complete, verified file is ready for submission.

This service is part of our end-to-end journey — see all five services on the homepage services section.
