Consultation & Guidance

Personalized academic guidance to choose the right major, city, and university for your goals and budget.

Choosing where and what to study in China is the single most important decision of your journey — and the easiest one to get wrong alone. Our advisors start from your profile: your grades, your budget, your career goals, and your family's expectations.

In a free consultation, we map the realistic options across our partner universities: which majors fit you, which cities match your lifestyle and budget, and which programs offer genuine scholarship potential. You leave the call with a clear, honest plan — not a sales pitch.

What's included

1Profile assessment

A full review of your academic record, language level, and goals to define what is realistically achievable.

2Major & university shortlist

A curated shortlist of programs and universities that fit your profile — including scholarship potential for each.

3Budget planning

A transparent cost breakdown per option: tuition, dormitory, insurance, and monthly living costs.

4Family briefing

We answer parents' questions directly — safety, follow-up, and how we support students after arrival.

How it works

  1. Book your free consultation by WhatsApp, phone, or the contact form.
  2. Share your transcripts and goals with your advisor.
  3. Receive your personalized study plan and program shortlist.
  4. Decide with full clarity — then we start your application.

This service is part of our end-to-end journey — see all five services on the homepage services section.
